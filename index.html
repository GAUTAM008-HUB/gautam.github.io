Background Information

"Forest Adventure" , located at Bedok Reservoir Park, is Singapore's first and only treetop obstacle course. It was opened on 25 August 2007 by the National Parks Board (NParks) in collaboration with Forest Adventure Pte Ltd with the support of Public Utilities Board (PUB). It was entirely rebuilt in 2017 and the newly renovated facility comprises of one additional rope course, making it a total of 3 age appropriate rope courses. It is also 40% larger currently compared to the past.
